
Movie Analytics Project: Real-World Scenario

Business Scenario:
A movie production company wants to improve its strategic decision-making by analyzing historical movie data. 
By examining patterns in box office performance, audience ratings, and production budgets, they aim to identify:
- Most successful genres, directors, and actors.
- Optimal movie release times.
- Target audience preferences based on box office and ratings data.

The company wants to use this analysis to:
- Decide on future movie projects.
- Improve movie marketing strategies.
- Streamline budgeting for upcoming productions.

Key Data Insights They Want:
- Top-grossing movies per year.
- Top actors and directors based on performance.
- Most profitable genres.
- Most frequent collaborations (actor-director partnerships).
- Average rating by genre.
